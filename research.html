Digital infrastructure for
evidence-informed policy-making

We build software that employs machine learning and natural language processing to evaluate online campaigns and monitor social media. Our goal is to build a unique analytics platform that guards and strengthens democratic dialogue.

Microexperiments in global health policy

We design and conduct frugal experiments targeted at identifying unique opportunities in the space of health policy in low and middle-income countries. Using evidence and careful reasoning, we produce analytical input to inform public opinion and policy-makers on important issues which, if properly addressed, could save thousands of years of life in good health.
