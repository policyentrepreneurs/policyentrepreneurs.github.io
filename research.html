Digital infrastructure for evidence-informed policy

We build infrastructure to identify neglected areas of public health policy in low- and middle-income countries in which change is likely to take place. We then work to establish outlier strategies that are likely to save lives at a relatively low price.

Microexperiments in public health advocacy

We build coalitions and use a broad repertoire of online advocacy tools to make change more likely to happen to save lives and improve public health situation in low- and middle-income countries worldwide.
