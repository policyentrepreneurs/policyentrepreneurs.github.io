Microexperiments in global health policy

We design and conduct frugal experiments targeted at identifying unique opportunities in the space of health policy in low and middle-income countries. Using evidence and careful reasoning, we produce analytical input intended to promptly inform policy-makers on issues that bear the most weight, and plausible ways to address them.
